Common Questions

Before You Contact Us

Can I see a demo before purchasing?

Yes. Contact us and we can walk you through a platform demo and show you a sample of the lead data available for your product category.

Do you offer custom or tailored plans?

For large enterprises or trading houses with specific requirements beyond our standard plans, we can discuss a custom arrangement. Contact our sales team directly.

How quickly can I access leads after payment?

Your membership is activated within 24 working hours of confirmed payment. Lead access begins immediately upon activation. Your RM contacts you within 48 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods including UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), NEFT/RTGS bank transfer, debit/credit cards, and net banking.

Is my contact information shared with buyers?

Only if you subscribe to the Business Promotion service. Your business name and product categories are shared with relevant buyers. Full contact details are shared only upon mutual interest expressed by the buyer.

Can I upgrade my plan mid-year?

Yes. You may upgrade to a higher plan at any time by paying the pro-rated difference. Downgrading to a lower plan during an active subscription period is not permitted.
