Overview

What is GST Registration & Why You Need It

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is India's unified indirect tax system that replaced multiple state and central taxes. GST registration is mandatory for any business with an annual turnover exceeding ₹20 lakhs (₹10 lakhs for special category states) and for all businesses engaged in interstate trade or export-import activities — irrespective of turnover.

For exporters, a GSTIN (GST Identification Number) is not just a legal compliance requirement — it is your gateway to claiming Input Tax Credit (ITC), issuing GST-compliant tax invoices, registering on official export promotion platforms, applying for IGST refunds on exports, and engaging in formal business-to-business transactions with credibility.

Documents Required

Documents Checklist by Entity Type

Proprietorship / Individual

  • PAN Card of the proprietor
  • Aadhaar Card (linked with mobile)
  • Photograph (passport size)
  • Business address proof (electricity bill / rent agreement)
  • Cancelled cheque / bank passbook
  • Digital Signature Certificate (if turnover > ₹5 crore)

Company / LLP / Partnership

  • PAN Card of the company/LLP
  • Certificate of Incorporation / Partnership Deed
  • Board Resolution / Authorisation Letter
  • PAN & Aadhaar of all directors/partners
  • Registered office address proof
  • Company bank account details

Pro Tip: All documents must be clearly scanned in PDF format (file size under 1MB each). Blurred, incomplete or mismatched documents are the primary reason for application rejection. Our team pre-verifies all documents before submission to minimise risk.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GST registration free on the government portal?+
Yes. The Government of India charges no fee for GST registration on the official GST portal (gst.gov.in). BuyGenix Solutions charges a professional facilitation fee only for our documentation assistance, form filling, and end-to-end support service. The government fee remains nil.
Do I need GST registration to export from India?+
Yes, GST registration is mandatory for exporters. Exports are treated as 'zero-rated' supplies under GST, meaning you can export without paying GST and claim refunds on input taxes. Without a GSTIN, you cannot issue valid tax invoices for exports or claim IGST refunds.
What if my application is rejected or a clarification is raised?+
Our service covers one complete application cycle including responding to any clarification notices (SCN — Show Cause Notice) issued by the GST officer within the normal processing period. We help you draft the appropriate response and resubmit within prescribed timelines at no additional charge.
How many GST registrations can a business have?+
A business operating in multiple states must register separately in each state it has a business presence. Additionally, a business can opt for multiple registrations within the same state for different business verticals. Our team can advise on the optimal registration structure for your operations.
Is BuyGenix Solutions a government-affiliated body?+
No. BuyGenix Solutions is a private B2B services company. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or a representative of the GST Council, GSTN, or any government authority. We act solely as a private facilitation service to assist businesses in navigating the application process.
