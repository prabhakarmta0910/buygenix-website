Privacy
Policy

Your privacy is of paramount importance to BuyGenix Solutions. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, protect, and process your personal and business information when you interact with our platform or services.

Last Updated: December 2024 | Version 2.0 | Applicable to all users of buygenixsolutions.com

Our Privacy Commitment: BuyGenix Solutions is committed to protecting your personal information and handling it with transparency, integrity, and respect. We collect only the data necessary to deliver our services, we do not sell your personal data to advertisers or unrelated third parties, and we implement reasonable technical and organisational safeguards to protect what you share with us.

Section 2

How We Collect Information

Information is collected through the following means:

  • Direct Submission: When you fill our enquiry forms, purchase a membership, submit documents for GST registration, or communicate with our team via email or WhatsApp.
  • Account Registration: When you create a member profile on our platform, you provide foundational identity and business information.
  • Automatic Collection: Our web server and analytics tools automatically collect technical information such as IP address, browser data, and browsing behaviour on our website through cookies and server logs.
  • Third-Party Sources: We may receive information about your business from publicly available sources such as the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), trade directories, GSTIN portal, and other government databases to verify your business credentials.
  • Referral Information: If you were referred by an existing member, we receive your contact information from the referring party to process the referral.
Section 3

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  1. Service Delivery: Providing the services you have subscribed to, including lead access, catalogue publication, RM assignment, and SEO/SMO execution.
  2. Account Management: Creating, maintaining, and managing your membership account, issuing invoices, and processing renewals.
  3. Communication: Sending service updates, lead notifications, membership reminders, support responses, and business opportunity alerts relevant to your membership.
  4. Business Matching: Sharing your business profile with relevant buyers as part of the business promotion service included in qualifying plans.
  5. Legal Compliance: Meeting our obligations under applicable laws including GST compliance, income tax, and information security regulations.
  6. Platform Improvement: Analysing usage patterns to improve platform features, service quality, and user experience.
  7. Fraud Prevention: Monitoring for suspicious activity, unauthorised data access, and referral fraud.
  8. Dispute Resolution: Maintaining records for the purpose of resolving any service disputes or legal claims.

We Do NOT Use Your Data For: Selling to advertisers, profiling for unrelated marketing, sharing with data brokers, or any purpose not expressly stated above. Your information is not monetised beyond the scope of delivering our stated services.

Section 4

Information Sharing & Third-Party Disclosure

BuyGenix Solutions does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We share information only in the following limited, necessary, and lawful circumstances:

  • With Buyers (Promotion Service): As part of the business promotion service, your business name, product categories, and contact details may be shared with buyers on our platform who have relevant procurement requirements. This is an expected and core part of the service you subscribe to.
  • With Service Partners: We engage trusted technology partners (hosting, email delivery, analytics) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality and data processing agreements. These partners are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.
  • With Government Authorities: We will disclose information to law enforcement, regulatory bodies, or courts when legally required to do so by a valid court order, government direction, or applicable law.
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our business, your information may be transferred to the acquirer as part of the business assets, subject to equivalent privacy protections.
  • With Your Consent: In any other circumstance, we will seek your explicit prior consent before sharing your information with any third party.
Section 5

Lead Database — Data Sources & Compliance

Given that our core service involves providing buyer lead information, this section specifically addresses how that data is sourced and managed:

  • Source of Lead Data: Buyer information in our database is compiled from publicly available sources including government trade portals, export-import directories, business registration databases, trade fair participation lists, voluntary enquiry submissions, and publicly accessible business profiles.
  • No Covert Collection: We do not collect lead data through covert means, data breaches, unauthorised access, or purchase from unverified data brokers.
  • Opt-Out for Buyers: Any buyer or business entity whose information appears in our database may request removal by contacting us at privacy@buygenixsolutions.com. We will process removal requests within 15 working days.
  • Data Freshness: While we endeavour to maintain current and accurate lead data, buyer information is inherently subject to change. Members must exercise their own due diligence before engaging with any lead contact.
  • Member Responsibility: Members who access lead data are independently responsible for ensuring their outreach communications comply with applicable anti-spam laws, telemarketing regulations, and the Telecom Commercial Communications Customer Preference Regulations (TCCCPR).
Section 6

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and collect analytical data. The following types of cookies may be active on our platform:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled as they are necessary for security and session management.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with our website (page views, traffic sources, user journeys). We may use Google Analytics or similar tools. This data is aggregated and anonymised where possible.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences to personalise your experience on return visits.
  • Marketing Cookies: If you have engaged with our social media presence (Instagram: @buygenixsolutions, Facebook: buygenixsolutions), those platforms may place cookies as per their own privacy policies.

You may disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings at any time. However, disabling cookies may affect certain platform functionality. Continued use of our website without changing your cookie settings constitutes consent to our cookie use.

Section 7

Data Retention

We retain your personal and business information for the following periods:

  • Active Members: All account information is retained for the full duration of the active membership and for a period of three (3) years following membership expiry for audit, legal, and dispute resolution purposes.
  • GST Registration Documents: Identity and compliance documents submitted for GST registration are retained for one (1) year post-service completion and then permanently deleted from our systems, unless required to be retained longer under applicable law.
  • Communication Records: Email and WhatsApp communications with our team are retained for a period of two (2) years for quality assurance and dispute resolution purposes.
  • Payment Records: Transaction records are retained for a minimum of seven (7) years to comply with financial and tax record-keeping requirements under Indian law.
  • Inactive/Cancelled Accounts: Upon account cancellation or membership expiry, your active profile data is archived within 90 days. You may request permanent deletion of non-legally-required data by contacting our privacy team.
Section 8

Data Security Measures

BuyGenix Solutions implements reasonable and appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorised personnel only, on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure, password-protected internal systems with two-factor authentication for administrative access
  • Regular security reviews and updates to our technical infrastructure
  • Confidentiality obligations imposed on all employees, contractors, and service partners who handle personal data

Security Limitation Notice: No method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that materially affects your information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

Section 9

Your Rights & Choices

As a user of BuyGenix Solutions, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you at any time by contacting our privacy team.
  • Right to Correction: You may request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information in your account profile.
  • Right to Deletion: You may request deletion of your personal data subject to our legal retention obligations. Note that deletion requests cannot be honoured for data we are required to retain under applicable law or for data tied to active service obligations.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Marketing: You may opt out of receiving marketing communications at any time by using the unsubscribe link in our emails or by contacting us directly. Note that service-related communications necessary for your membership cannot be opted out of during active membership.
  • Right to Portability: You may request your data in a structured, commonly used format where technically feasible.
  • Right to Object: You may object to specific processing activities where our processing is based on legitimate interests rather than contract or legal obligation.

To exercise any of the above rights, please contact us at privacy@buygenixsolutions.com with your registered email address and the specific request. We will respond within 30 working days.

Section 10

Children's Privacy

BuyGenix Solutions is a B2B business platform intended exclusively for business entities and individuals who are at least 18 years of age and legally authorised to enter into binding commercial agreements. We do not knowingly collect, process, or retain any personal information from persons under the age of 18. If we become aware that a person under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will take immediate steps to delete such information from our records. If you believe a minor has submitted information to us, please contact us immediately at privacy@buygenixsolutions.com.

Section 11

Changes to This Privacy Policy

BuyGenix Solutions reserves the right to update, modify, or revise this Privacy Policy at any time to reflect changes in our practices, services, legal obligations, or applicable regulations. When we make material changes to this Policy, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Post a prominent notice on our website for a minimum of 30 days
  • Send an email notification to active members where the change materially affects how their data is processed

Your continued use of BuyGenix Solutions' platform or services after the effective date of any revision constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed of how we protect your information.
