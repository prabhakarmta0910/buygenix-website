Buy Leads — Indian & International Buyers

Our flagship service. Access a curated, verified database of active buyers sourcing products from India — both domestically and internationally across 50+ countries.

🇮🇳 Indian Buyer Leads Include:

  • Verified company name, type, and industry sector
  • Decision-maker contact: name, phone, WhatsApp, email
  • Specific product requirements with quantity and frequency
  • City, state, and regional targeting filters
  • Monthly buying volume and import history indicators
  • Lead freshness rating (date of last verification)

🌍 International Buyer Leads Include:

  • Country-wise categorised overseas buyer profiles
  • Company name, website, and LinkedIn profile
  • Import category, volume range, and sourcing history
  • Direct contact email and LinkedIn connection details
  • Coverage: USA, UK, UAE, Europe, Southeast Asia, Australia
  • New international leads added to database monthly

Unlike generic B2B platforms where you're on your own, BuyGenix Solutions assigns you an individual Relationship Manager who stays with you throughout your membership — your personal export growth partner.

  • Full onboarding: profile setup, catalogue creation, business card activation
  • Proactive lead matching based on your specific product categories
  • Sharing your business details with relevant incoming buyer enquiries
  • Monthly performance reports: lead views, catalogue traffic, buyer interest
  • Priority WhatsApp and email access to your assigned RM
  • Assistance in responding to buyer enquiries professionally
  • Coordination of GST, SEO/SMO and any other subscribed services
