Who We Are

Built by Exporters, for Exporters

BuyGenix Solutions is an India-based B2B digital platform designed specifically for the export-import trade community. We understand the real challenges exporters face: finding reliable buyers, maintaining a professional digital presence, navigating compliance requirements, and building trust with overseas clients.

Our platform combines buyer lead intelligence, digital marketing, professional cataloguing, and personalised support — all in one affordable membership — so that exporters of every size can compete and grow in the global market.

We are a private digital services company. We are not affiliated with any government body, trade ministry, or official export promotion council. We operate as an independent B2B marketplace facilitator.
