Who We Are

A Platform Built by Exporters, for Exporters

BuyGenix Solutions is an India-based B2B digital platform designed specifically for the export-import trade community. We understand the real challenges exporters face every day: finding reliable international and domestic buyers, maintaining a professional digital presence, navigating compliance requirements, and building trust with overseas clients.

Our platform combines buyer lead intelligence, digital marketing, professional cataloguing, and personalised relationship manager support — all in one affordable annual membership — so that exporters of every size can compete confidently in the global marketplace.

We are headquartered in Delhi and our team works entirely in service of one goal: making Indian exporters globally competitive.
