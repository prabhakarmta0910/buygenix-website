Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive.

How quickly will I receive buyer leads?
After your membership is activated, your Relationship Manager will reach out within 24 hours. Your first leads are typically delivered within 3–5 working days of activation.
Can I upgrade my plan later?
Yes — you can upgrade from Starter to Growth, Professional, or Enterprise at any time. Your remaining membership period is pro-rated against the new plan cost.
How are buyer leads verified?
Every lead is manually reviewed by our team before delivery. We verify the buyer company, their product requirements, and their contact details before sending them to you.
Do I need an IEC code to join?
No — you can join without an IEC code. We will help you apply for your IEC and other registrations as part of your membership. You can receive leads while your IEC application is in progress.
What categories of products do you cover?
We cover 12+ export categories including textiles, spices, agricultural products, handicrafts, engineering goods, garments, leather, chemicals, gems and more.
Is there a refund policy?
Please refer to our Terms & Conditions for full details of our refund and cancellation policy. For specific queries, contact us on WhatsApp before purchasing.
