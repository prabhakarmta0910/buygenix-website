Why GST Matters for Exporters

Your GST Registration Is the First Step to Exporting Legally

Under Indian law, any business with an annual turnover exceeding ₹20 lakhs (₹10 lakhs in special category states) must register under GST. However, for exporters, GST registration is mandatory regardless of turnover since exports are classified as inter-state supply.

With GST registration, your business becomes eligible to claim IGST refunds on exports, register for a Letter of Undertaking (LUT) to export without paying tax, and gain instant credibility with international buyers who verify Indian exporters before placing orders.

Our team handles the documentation, portal submission, and follow-up — all you need to do is provide us the required documents.

Benefits

Why Every Exporter Needs GST Registration

💰

IGST Refund on Exports

Registered exporters can claim refunds on IGST paid on inputs used in export goods, significantly reducing your cost of production and improving profitability.

📜

LUT — Export Without Tax

With a GST registration, you can file a Letter of Undertaking (LUT) and export goods and services without paying IGST at the time of export — improving cash flow significantly.

🌍

International Buyer Trust

Buyers in Europe, USA, and the Middle East verify GSTIN before placing orders. A registered GSTIN instantly establishes your business as legally compliant and credible.

🏦

Bank Account & IEC Eligibility

A valid GSTIN is required to open a current account, apply for an IEC (Importer Exporter Code), and avail ECGC export credit insurance — all essential for international trade.

📊

Input Tax Credit (ITC)

Claim credit for GST paid on raw materials, services, and capital goods used in your business operations, reducing your overall tax liability and improving margins.

📋

Formal Business Recognition

GST registration gives your business formal legal recognition, making it easier to participate in government tenders, trade exhibitions, and export promotion schemes.

Documents Required

Documents vary by business type. Below is the standard list for sole proprietors and private limited companies:

✓ PAN Card of business / proprietor
✓ Aadhaar Card of authorised signatory
✓ Photograph of authorised signatory

FAQs

Common GST Questions from Exporters

Is GST registration mandatory for all exporters?

Yes. Under GST law, exports are treated as inter-state supply, making GST registration mandatory for all exporters regardless of annual turnover. Even if your total revenue is below ₹20 lakhs, you must register if you are exporting goods or services.

How long does GST registration take?

Typically 7 to 15 working days from the date of successful submission of your complete application on the GSTN portal. Delays can occur if additional documents are requested by the tax officer. Our team follows up on your behalf throughout.

Can I export without GST registration?

Technically, some exports under exemptions are possible, but practically, most buyers and freight forwarders require a valid GSTIN. Additionally, without GST registration you cannot claim IGST refunds or file for LUT, significantly impacting your export profitability.

What is the difference between IGST and LUT for exporters?

When exporting with payment of IGST, you pay tax upfront and claim a refund later. Under LUT (Letter of Undertaking), you can export without paying IGST, avoiding the cash flow burden. LUT requires a valid GST registration and is renewable annually.

Does BuyGenix provide GST filing or just registration?

BuyGenix Solutions assists with GST registration only — the one-time process of obtaining your GSTIN. Ongoing GST return filing (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B etc.) is a separate service not included in our membership. We recommend engaging a chartered accountant for compliance filing.
