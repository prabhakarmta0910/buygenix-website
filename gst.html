Why Registrations Matter for Your Business

Every stage of running and growing an export business needs paperwork — from the PAN that opens your first bank account, to the GST and IEC that let you raise invoices and ship goods, to the board-specific certificates that let you export spices, marine products, tea, or agricultural goods to specific markets.

Getting any one of these wrong, late, or skipped entirely can delay a shipment, block a payment, or get an application rejected outright. Our team handles the paperwork end-to-end so you can focus on running your business.

Documents You'll Typically Need

Every registration above has its own exact checklist, but most start with the same core set of documents. For an individual or proprietorship, this usually means:

🪪PAN Card of the owner / business
🏠Proof of business address
🏦Bank statement / cancelled cheque
📄Aadhaar card of the authorised signatory
🖼Passport-size photograph
📋Business registration certificate (if any)
✍Authorisation letter (for companies / firms)
📑Digital Signature Certificate (for companies)

Board-specific registrations (APEDA, Spices Board, MPEDA, Tea/Coffee/Tobacco Board) and certificates (COO, Phytosanitary, Fumigation) need a few extra product or facility-specific documents. Contact us and we'll send you the exact checklist for whichever registration you need.
