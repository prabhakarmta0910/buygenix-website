What is GST Registration?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) registration is mandatory for businesses whose turnover exceeds ₹20 lakhs (₹10 lakhs in special category states), and is essential for all exporters who want to claim zero-rated export benefits and Input Tax Credit (ITC) refunds.

As an exporter, a valid GSTIN (GST Identification Number) is required to:

  • Raise tax invoices for export shipments
  • Claim full refunds of Input Tax Credit on exported goods
  • File shipping bills and customs declarations
  • Register on government export promotion portals
  • Open an export-linked current bank account
ℹ️ Exports are zero-rated under GST

You can export goods without paying GST and claim a full refund of any input tax already paid. GST registration is the essential first step to accessing this benefit.

All Export Registrations We Handle

GST Registration

Goods and Services Tax identification number for your business. Required for all exporters claiming zero-rated benefits and ITC refunds. Processing time: 3–7 working days.

  • Mandatory for all exporters claiming ITC refunds
  • Required for generating e-Way bills
  • Covers all categories: goods and services
  • Monthly or quarterly return filing thereafter

IEC — Import Export Code

The Import Export Code is a 10-digit identification number issued by DGFT (Directorate General of Foreign Trade). Mandatory for all exporters and importers — without an IEC, you cannot legally export or import goods from India. Processing time: 1–3 working days.

  • Mandatory for all goods exporters and importers
  • Required for customs clearance and foreign bank remittances
  • Lifetime validity — no annual renewal required
  • Issued by DGFT, Government of India

MSME / Udyam Registration

The Udyam Registration gives your business access to government subsidies, priority lending, export promotion schemes, and benefits under the MSME Development Act. Processing time: 1 working day.

  • Free registration on the government Udyam portal
  • Qualifies for MSME export development schemes
  • Priority sector lending and lower interest rates
  • Access to credit guarantee fund schemes

FSSAI Licence

The Food Safety and Standards Authority of India licence is mandatory for all food product exporters — including spices, rice, organic food, processed food, beverages, and agricultural goods. Processing time: 7–30 working days.

  • Mandatory for all food and agricultural exporters
  • Covers spices, rice, organic food, processed food, beverages
  • Required by international buyers and customs authorities
  • Annual renewal required

Documents Required for GST Registration

For an individual or proprietorship, the standard documents required are:

🪪PAN Card of the owner / business
🏠Proof of business address
🏦Bank statement / cancelled cheque
📄Aadhaar card of the authorised signatory
🖼Passport-size photograph
📋Business registration certificate (if any)
✍Authorisation letter (for companies / firms)
📑Digital Signature Certificate (for companies)

For other business types (partnership, LLP, private limited company), additional documents may be required. Contact us for a personalised checklist.
