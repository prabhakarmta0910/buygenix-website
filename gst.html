Overview

What is GST Registration & Why You Need It

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is India's unified indirect tax system. GST registration is mandatory for any business with an annual turnover exceeding ₹20 lakhs and for all businesses engaged in interstate trade or export-import activities — irrespective of turnover.

For exporters, a GSTIN is your gateway to claiming Input Tax Credit (ITC), issuing GST-compliant tax invoices, applying for IGST refunds on exports, and engaging in formal B2B transactions with credibility.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GST registration free on the government portal?+
Yes. The Government of India charges no fee for GST registration on the official portal (gst.gov.in). BuyGenix Solutions charges a professional facilitation fee only for our documentation assistance and end-to-end support. The government fee remains nil.
Do I need GST registration to export from India?+
Yes, GST registration is mandatory for exporters. Exports are treated as ‘zero-rated’ supplies under GST, meaning you can export without paying GST and claim refunds on input taxes. Without a GSTIN, you cannot issue valid tax invoices for exports.
What if my application receives a clarification notice?+
Our service covers one complete application cycle including responding to any Show Cause Notice (SCN) issued by the GST officer within the normal processing period. We help you draft the appropriate response at no additional charge.
Is BuyGenix Solutions a government-affiliated body?+
No. BuyGenix Solutions is a private B2B services company. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or a representative of the GST Council, GSTN, or any government authority. We act solely as a private facilitation service.
How long does the GSTIN remain valid?+
A GSTIN does not expire as long as the business continues to file GST returns and comply with applicable provisions. Cancellation occurs only upon voluntary surrender or government action for non-compliance.
