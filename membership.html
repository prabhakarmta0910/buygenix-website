Frequently Asked Questions

Can I see a demo before purchasing?
Yes. Contact us and we'll walk you through a live demo and show you a sample of the lead data available for your specific product category — no commitment required.
When do leads become available after I pay?
Your membership activates within 24 working hours of payment confirmation. Leads start arriving in real time via WhatsApp and email immediately upon activation. Your RM contacts you within 48 hours.
Do unused monthly leads carry forward?
No. Monthly lead credits are allocated on the 1st of each month and do not carry forward to the next month. Leads are delivered to you in real time — we recommend acting on them promptly for best results.
Can I upgrade my plan mid-year?
Yes. You can upgrade to a higher plan at any time by paying the pro-rated difference. Downgrading during an active subscription period is not permitted.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), NEFT/RTGS bank transfer, debit/credit cards, and net banking. All payments are processed securely.
Is BuyGenix a government-affiliated body?
No. BuyGenix Solutions is a private digital services company. We are not affiliated with any government body, trade ministry, or export promotion council. We operate independently.
