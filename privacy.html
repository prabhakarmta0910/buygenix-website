Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user and member of BuyGenix Solutions.

Contents

1. Information We Collect 2. How We Use Your Data 3. Data Sharing 4. Data Storage & Security 5. Cookies 6. Your Rights 7. Third-Party Services 8. Children's Privacy 9. Policy Changes 10. Contact Us

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Last Updated: April 2025

Applies To: BuyGenix Solutions platform and all services

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you contact us, register for membership, or use our services, we collect:

  • Full name and business name
  • Email address and phone or WhatsApp number
  • Business address and GSTIN (if provided)
  • Product categories and export or import interests
  • Payment information (processed via third-party payment gateways — we do not store card details)
  • Communications you send us via email, WhatsApp, or our contact form

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring website or traffic source
  • Device type (desktop or mobile)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and activate your membership
  • Deliver buyer leads relevant to your product category
  • Assign and facilitate communication with your Relationship Manager
  • Send your monthly performance reports and lead batches
  • Respond to your enquiries and support requests
  • Send service-related communications (activation, renewal reminders, updates)
  • Process referral commissions under the referral programme
  • Improve our platform, services, and buyer database quality
  • Comply with legal obligations applicable to us as a business

We do not use your data for unsolicited third-party advertising.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

BuyGenix Solutions does not sell your personal information to any third party.

We may share limited information with:

  • Service providers: Payment gateways, email service providers, and hosting companies who process data on our behalf under confidentiality agreements
  • Your Relationship Manager: Your contact details and business profile are shared with your assigned RM for the purpose of delivering membership services
  • Legal authorities: If required by law, court order, or government request

Any data sharing is limited to what is strictly necessary for service delivery.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on servers within India and protected using industry-standard security measures including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits.

We retain your personal data for the duration of your membership and for a period of 3 years thereafter, after which it is securely deleted or anonymised.

While we implement appropriate security measures, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and to contact us immediately if you suspect any unauthorised access to your account.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. We use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies: To understand how visitors use our website (aggregated, anonymous data)
  • Preference cookies: To remember your settings and preferences

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect some website functionality.

6. Your Rights

As a user of BuyGenix Solutions, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Objection: Object to specific uses of your data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at Info@buygenixsolutions.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Third-Party Services

Our website and services may contain links to third-party websites including Instagram, Facebook, and WhatsApp. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these platforms. We encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

Payment processing is handled by third-party payment gateways. We do not store your payment card details on our servers.

8. Children's Privacy

BuyGenix Solutions services are intended for business use by adults aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify active members of material changes via email. The updated policy will always be available on our website with the effective date clearly indicated.
