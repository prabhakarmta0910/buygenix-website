Privacy
Policy

Your privacy is of paramount importance to BuyGenix Solutions. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, protect, and process your personal and business information when you interact with our platform or services.

Last Updated: December 2024 | Version 2.0

Our Privacy Commitment: BuyGenix Solutions is committed to protecting your personal information and handling it with transparency, integrity, and respect. We collect only the data necessary to deliver our services, we do not sell your personal data to advertisers or unrelated third parties, and we implement reasonable technical and organisational safeguards to protect what you share with us.

Section 2

How We Collect Information

  • Direct Submission: When you fill our enquiry forms, purchase a membership, submit documents for GST registration, or communicate with our team.
  • Account Registration: When you create a member profile on our platform.
  • Automatic Collection: Our web server and analytics tools automatically collect technical information such as IP address, browser data, and browsing behaviour through cookies and server logs.
  • Third-Party Sources: We may receive information about your business from publicly available sources such as the MCA, trade directories, GSTIN portal, and other government databases to verify your business credentials.
  • Referral Information: If you were referred by an existing member, we receive your contact information from the referring party to process the referral.
Section 3

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  1. Service Delivery: Providing the services you have subscribed to, including lead access, catalogue publication, RM assignment, and SEO/SMO execution.
  2. Account Management: Creating, maintaining, and managing your membership account, issuing invoices, and processing renewals.
  3. Communication: Sending service updates, lead notifications, membership reminders, support responses, and business opportunity alerts.
  4. Business Matching: Sharing your business profile with relevant buyers as part of the business promotion service.
  5. Legal Compliance: Meeting our obligations under applicable laws including GST compliance and information security regulations.
  6. Platform Improvement: Analysing usage patterns to improve platform features, service quality, and user experience.
  7. Fraud Prevention: Monitoring for suspicious activity, unauthorised data access, and referral fraud.

We Do NOT Use Your Data For: Selling to advertisers, profiling for unrelated marketing, sharing with data brokers, or any purpose not expressly stated above. Your information is not monetised beyond the scope of delivering our stated services.

Section 4

Information Sharing & Third-Party Disclosure

BuyGenix Solutions does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We share information only in the following limited, necessary, and lawful circumstances:

  • With Buyers (Promotion Service): As part of the business promotion service, your business name, product categories, and contact details may be shared with relevant buyers.
  • With Service Partners: Trusted technology partners (hosting, email delivery, analytics) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • With Government Authorities: We will disclose information to law enforcement, regulatory bodies, or courts when legally required by a valid court order or applicable law.
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of our business, your information may be transferred to the acquirer subject to equivalent privacy protections.
  • With Your Consent: In any other circumstance, we will seek your explicit prior consent.
Section 5

Lead Database — Data Sources & Compliance

  • Source of Lead Data: Buyer information in our database is compiled from publicly available sources including government trade portals, export-import directories, business registration databases, trade fair participation lists, voluntary enquiry submissions, and publicly accessible business profiles.
  • No Covert Collection: We do not collect lead data through covert means, data breaches, or unauthorised access.
  • Opt-Out for Buyers: Any buyer or business entity whose information appears in our database may request removal by contacting us at privacy@buygenixsolutions.com. We will process removal requests within 15 working days.
  • Member Responsibility: Members who access lead data are independently responsible for ensuring their outreach communications comply with applicable anti-spam laws and the TCCCPR regulations.
Section 6

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. The following types of cookies may be active:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled as they are necessary for security and session management.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with our website. We may use Google Analytics or similar tools with aggregated, anonymised data.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences to personalise your experience on return visits.
  • Social Media Cookies: If you engage with our Instagram @buygenixsolutions or Facebook page, those platforms may place cookies as per their own privacy policies.

You may disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings. Continued use of our website without changing your cookie settings constitutes consent to our cookie use.

Section 7

Data Retention

  • Active Members: All account information is retained for the full duration of the active membership and for three (3) years following membership expiry.
  • GST Registration Documents: Identity and compliance documents are retained for one (1) year post-service completion and then permanently deleted.
  • Communication Records: Email and WhatsApp communications are retained for two (2) years for quality assurance and dispute resolution.
  • Payment Records: Transaction records are retained for a minimum of seven (7) years to comply with financial record-keeping requirements under Indian law.
Section 8

Data Security Measures

BuyGenix Solutions implements reasonable and appropriate technical and organisational security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorised personnel on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure, password-protected internal systems with two-factor authentication for administrative access
  • Confidentiality obligations imposed on all employees, contractors, and service partners who handle personal data

Security Limitation Notice: No method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that materially affects your information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

Section 9

Your Rights & Choices

  • Right to Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: You may request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Deletion: You may request deletion of your personal data subject to our legal retention obligations.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Marketing: You may opt out of receiving marketing communications at any time. Service-related communications cannot be opted out of during active membership.
  • Right to Object: You may object to specific processing activities where our processing is based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of the above rights, please contact us at privacy@buygenixsolutions.com. We will respond within 30 working days.

Section 10

Children's Privacy

BuyGenix Solutions is a B2B business platform intended exclusively for business entities and individuals who are at least 18 years of age and legally authorised to enter into binding commercial agreements. We do not knowingly collect, process, or retain any personal information from persons under the age of 18. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal information, we will take immediate steps to delete such information.

Section 11

Changes to This Privacy Policy

BuyGenix Solutions reserves the right to update, modify, or revise this Privacy Policy at any time. When we make material changes, we will update the “Last Updated” date, post a prominent notice on our website for a minimum of 30 days, and send an email notification to active members where the change materially affects how their data is processed. Your continued use of our platform after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
